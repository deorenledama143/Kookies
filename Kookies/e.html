About KOOKIE

KOOKIE is a student-built e-commerce project that celebrates the joy of homemade cookies. Every click should feel as warm as every bite — blending thoughtful design, clean code, and a sprinkle of creativity.

Our Mission

To make every moment sweeter by delivering freshly baked cookies made with care, accessible, simple, and full of warmth.

Our Vision

To become the go-to online cookie shop where every customer feels the love of small-batch baking and fast, friendly service.
